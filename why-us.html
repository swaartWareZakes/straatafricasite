WHY US

Why choose Straat Africa

At Straat Africa, we understand that real connection doesn’t happen behind a screen or on a billboard in isolation. It happens out there — on the move, in taxis, in transit, and in the everyday rhythm of South Africa’s cities and townships.

We turn this daily movement into opportunity — transforming transit and public spaces into meaningful media platforms that brands can trust for reach, relevance, and results.

With our data-driven approach, creative strategy, and deep understanding of urban culture, Straat Africa gives your brand more than exposure — it gives it presence.

That’s the power of Media in Motion.

UNMATCHED REACH

85% of South Africa’s working class relies on taxis

That’s over 19 million daily passengers. Our in-taxi screens give your brand a direct line to this powerful audience.

STREET-LEVEL RELEVANCE

We operate in the spaces where real life unfolds

We connect your brand to people through culture, conversation, and movement.

MULTI-PLATFORM IMPACT

From Taxi TV and Transit Media to Digital Out-of-Home, Brand Activations, and Community Engagement — we integrate creativity with strategy to make every impression count.

SMART TARGETING & DATA INSIGHT

With location-based technology and audience analytics (powered by partners like Moving Walls and Sebenza WiFi), we make sure your brand appears in the right place, at the right time.
