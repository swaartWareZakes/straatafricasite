WE BRING MEDIA TO THE STREETS.

At Straat Africa, we transform everyday taxis into powerful, moving media platforms that connect brands to millions of South Africans — in motion, every day. Our screens live where life happens: on the road, in conversation, in the heartbeat of our cities and townships.

We believe the streets aren’t just spaces people pass through. They’re where stories travel, culture spreads, and brands become part of the journey. That’s why we call it Media in Motion — because we don’t just move messages, we move with the people.

WHAT WE DO

We specialise in connecting brands to real audiences through movement, culture, and innovation. From the streets to the screens, every service we offer is built to meet people where they are — in motion.

Our work spans Taxi TV Advertising, Transit Media, and Digital Out-Of-Home, supported by smart Location-Based Targeting that ensures every message hits the right place at the right time.

Our team also drives Brand Activations, Creative Strategy, and Community Engagement, turning every touchpoint into an experience that moves both people and brands forward.

Because in the world of Straat Africa, movement isn’t just what we do. It’s what we believe in.

OUR CLIENTS

From household names to homegrown heroes, we’ve partnered with brands that believe in the power of the street.

Each campaign we run is more than media placement — it’s a chance to connect with real people in motion, across the cities and townships that shape South African life.

Whether it’s food, fashion, entertainment, or social impact, our clients trust Straat Africa to take their message where it matters most — to the people, on the move, every day.

OUR PARTNERS

Partnership is at the heart of how we move. Together with innovative partners like Moving Walls and Sebenza WiFi, we’re building smarter, more connected ways to deliver media across South Africa.

From advanced data and analytics to real-time digital connectivity, our partners help us keep Straat Africa at the forefront of street-level media — ensuring every message moves with purpose, precision, and impact.
